## For the end user

Register candidates and clients through structured wizards that capture all UK regulatory requirements.

### Candidate wizard (6 steps)

| Step | What it captures |
|---|---|
| Personal Details | Name, contact, NI number, DOB |
| Experience & Skills | Sector, skills, work history |
| Right to Work | Immigration Act 2006 — document type, verification, expiry |
| DBS Check | Level, certificate number, Update Service status |
| GDPR & Data Protection | Privacy notice, marketing, client sharing, retention consent |
| Emergency & Health | Emergency contact, adjustments (Equality Act 2010), equal ops |

### Client wizard (4 steps)

| Step | What it captures |
|---|---|
| Company Details | Company name/number, contact, sector |
| IR35 & Status | Company size (for off-payroll rules), SDS process |
| Terms of Business | Fee %, rebate period, payment terms, exclusivity |
| Compliance & GDPR | Insurance confirmation (PI/PL/EL), data protection contact |

### Key rules

- **Immigration Act 2006**: RTW verification before engagement, £45K penalty
- **DBS**: regulated activity roles require enhanced checks
- **UK GDPR**: privacy notice, explicit consent for special category data
- **Conduct Regulations 2003**: written terms, suitability checks
- **IR35**: company size determination for off-payroll status
